About Jacq

I am a self-taught designer with experience in visual, UI/UX, product, and web design. For the past six years, I worked on early-stage mobile and web marketplace apps in an operations, marketing and design capacity. My experience in cross-functional roles in a startup environment has opened my eyes to a design and engineering career path. As an aspiring full-stack designer, I strive to come up with elegant solutions to complex problems — from ideation to implementation.

When I’m not obsessing over pixels in Sketch, my interests include traveling, seeing live music (particularly hip hop), and scouring thrift stores.
